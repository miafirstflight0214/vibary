Ring a DingDing illustration studio was established in 2020.

Ding Ding lives and works in Beijing, China. Her illustrations are vibrant, detail-rich and narrative-driven — every piece tells a story that rewards close looking.

She works across film posters, editorial illustration, book covers, package design and brand identity.
